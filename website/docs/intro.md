# Introduction

Welcome to **Slides Plus**, a powerful presentation plugin for Obsidian that brings the best of [Slidev](https://sli.dev/) to your note-taking workflow.

## What is Slides Plus?

Slides Plus transforms your Markdown notes into beautiful, interactive presentations. Write your slides in Markdown, preview them live, and present directly from your vault.
